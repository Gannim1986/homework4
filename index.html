QUIZ GAME


JavaScript code Quiz game.


Instructions


How to Play

Click the Start button to begin the game. Once clicked Timer will start immediately. you will have 60 seconds to answer all question. Read questions carefully and select one of the correct answer out of 4 options. Once you get the correct answer your score will go up by 1 point and next question will be display. If you get Incorrect answer it will decrease your time by 15 seconds. once you have completed the quiz you will have to enter your Name and scores will be displayed. You can Restart the game by clicking on restart button.
